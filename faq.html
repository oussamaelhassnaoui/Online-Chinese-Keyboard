Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is your Chinese keyboard tool really free to use?

Yes, our Chinese keyboard tool is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees for accessing or using our virtual Chinese keyboard, Pinyin converter, or any other features on our website.

Do I need to install any software to use your Chinese keyboard?

No, our Chinese keyboard is a web-based tool that works directly in your browser. You don't need to install any software, apps, or extensions. Simply visit our website and start typing in Chinese immediately.

How does your Pinyin to Chinese conversion work?

Our Pinyin to Chinese converter uses the CC-CEDICT dictionary, which contains over 120,000 Chinese words and phrases. When you enter Pinyin, our system matches it against the dictionary entries and displays the corresponding Chinese characters.

Can I use your tool on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our Chinese keyboard is fully responsive and works on all devices, including smartphones and tablets. We also have a dedicated mobile version optimized for touchscreens.

Do you support both Simplified and Traditional Chinese characters?

Yes, our tool supports both Simplified and Traditional Chinese characters. You can switch between character sets as needed for your specific requirements.

Is there a limit to how much text I can type or convert?

No, there are no limits on the amount of text you can type or convert using our tool. You can type as much Chinese text as you need.

Technical Questions

Do you store my typed text or conversions?

No, we do not store any of your typed text or conversions on our servers. All processing happens in your browser, and your data is not saved or transmitted to our servers.

Why am I seeing incorrect characters when I convert Pinyin?

This can happen when multiple Chinese characters share the same Pinyin pronunciation. Try adding tone marks to your Pinyin input to improve accuracy, or check if there are alternative characters in the suggestions list.

Can I use your tool offline?

Our tool requires an internet connection to load initially, but once loaded, most features will work offline since the processing happens in your browser. However, some features like real-time suggestions may require an internet connection.

Which browsers are supported?

Our Chinese keyboard works on all modern browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser.
