Chinese Stroke Keyboard - Write Chinese Characters by Stroke

Our online Chinese stroke keyboard allows you to write Chinese characters by drawing stroke patterns. This tool is perfect for learning proper stroke order and practicing Chinese character writing.

How to Use This Stroke Keyboard

Using our online stroke keyboard is simple:

  • Draw Chinese characters using the stroke input panel
  • Follow the correct stroke order for each character
  • Recognized characters will appear in the text area above
  • Copy your text with the "Copy Text" button
  • Search directly on Google or YouTube with your Chinese text
